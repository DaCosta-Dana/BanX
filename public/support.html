Frequently Asked Questions

Shelly J.

How do I add a new beneficiary?

To add a beneficiary, navigate to the 'Payments' section, click on 'New Transaction', and then click on 'Add beneficiary' fill in the required details, and submit.

How do I view my spending analytics?

Visit the 'Analytics' section to see categorized spending details and overall spending graphs.

How do I schedule recurring payments?

In the 'Payments' section, select the date of the recurring payment in the calendar and fill in the required details and then submit.

Can I update my profile information?

Yes, go to 'Settings' and edit the details in the profile section.
