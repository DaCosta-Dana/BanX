BanX is a simulation of a digital banking platform that enables users to manage their finances, make payments, and analyze their spending with ease.

To create a new transaction, navigate to the Payments section and click on "New Transaction." Fill in the required details and submit the form.

To reset your password, go to the Settings section and select "Change Password." Follow the instructions to securely update your credentials.

You can reach our support team at support@banx.com for assistance.

Recurring payments allow you to set up automated transactions on a specified day of the month. You can configure them in the Payments section.
